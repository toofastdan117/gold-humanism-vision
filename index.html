Purpose

Baylor College of Medicine's Ophthalmology Interest Group has a eye care initiative that is funded by the The Arnold P. Gold Foundation.

We perform vision screening and eye exams to underserved clients at community health fairs. If we determine that a client is in need of seeing a qualified ophthalmologist or optometrist, we recommend the following clinics. All of the following clinics accept patients without insurance.
