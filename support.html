Grant Application Steps

2-3 Months Before Grant Deadline

  1. Email faculty mentors at Baylor College of Medicine (BCM) and McGovern Medical School. Previous mentors include Drs. Dana Clark, Ravi Chundru, and Andrew Lee.
  2. Email various site mentors to confirm support and dates for health fair events. Previous health fair sites include: Open Gate Homeless Ministries, Casa Maria, Día De La Mujer Latina, and HOMES Clinic.
  3. Email Prevent Blindness Texas to confirm they have vision screening machines available on the days of the health fairs. Get a vision screener to operate the vision screening machine.
  4. Write a draft of the grant! Make sure to follow the guidelines and address each section described on the Gold Humanism webpage.
  5. Reach out to BCM/McGovern deans of student affairs to gain a letter of support, stating the you are in good academic standing. The letter should also state support for the choice of faculty mentors, site locations, and declare that the school is willing to accept the funds if the applicants are awarded.
  6. Fill out Baylor's online Biomedical Research and Assurance Information Network (BRAIN) at least 10 days before submission is due!
